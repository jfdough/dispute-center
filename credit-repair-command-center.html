CROA-Compliant Contracts
Generate and track written agreements per federal and Texas law.
📋 Federal & Texas Requirements Every client requires: (1) Written contract before services begin, (2) "Consumer Credit File Rights" disclosure, (3) Signed 3-day right to cancel, (4) No upfront fees — payment only after deletion/service delivery. Texas Chapter 393 Finance Code additionally requires CSO registration, $10,000 surety bond, and specific contract language.
